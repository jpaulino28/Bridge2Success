Our Mission

Bridge 2 Success (B2S) is a student-led college access organization committed to closing the opportunity gap for first-generation and underrepresented high school students.

Our mission is to bridge the information gap that prevents many talented students from reaching their full potential. By providing mentorship from students who have successfully navigated the college admissions process, we empower scholars with the knowledge, confidence, and support needed to pursue their goals and thrive at top colleges and universities. At Bridge 2 Success, we are building a community where opportunity is shared, potential is recognized, and success is within reach for every student.

Access

Connecting students with mentors, resources, and opportunities they may not know exist.

Education

Teaching the full application process — essays, testing, financial aid, and more! Our goal is to help students feel confident and prepared as they navigate the college admissions process.

Community

Building lasting networks of students and mentors across the United States.

Excellence

Encouraging students to aim for schools that match their full potential and help them achieve their academic and personal goals.
